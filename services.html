What is your interest?

Theatre

If every performance is a work of art, then it must be said that every stage is a fresh canvas on which to fill with a work of art. The ‘set’ or ‘setting’ is the design in which we immerse our story perspective.
The impressions expressed on the set give us a time, a place.
We provide tools in the form of materials and the professi- onal creatives, trained with the equipments, who are there to guide, support and stimulate the practical learning of the participants as they discover the materials and embark on creating towards a group defined goal.
Hip-hop theater is a the stage where stories use one or more of the four elements of hip-hop culture—B-boying, Graffiti writing, MCing (rapping), and DJing. Other cultural markers of hip-hop such as spoken word, beatboxing, and hip-hop dance can be included as well although they are not always present. What is most important is the language of the theatrical piece and the plot’s relevance to the world.
Hip-hop theatre must fit into the realm of theatrical performance, and it must be by, about and for the hip-hop generation, participants in hip-hop culture, or both.”
Danny Hoch, founder of the Hip-Hop Theater Festival

Dance

Breakdancing is a hip-hop dance with really cool poses, styles and spins. From South Bronx, New York in the 1970’s breakdance moves take time to master, especially the ”down rock” moves.
“Many people with roots in hip-hop culture feel that hip-hop dancing should not be formally ”taught.” They feel that teaching specific moves takes away from the originality factor that hip-hop possesses...” Bedinghaus, Treva from ”A Few Things You Should Know About Hip-Hop Dancing.” ThoughtCo, Aug. 22, 2019, thoughtco.com/hip-hop-dance-1007434.
With HTK we understand the changing world and the constant requirements to ‘fit-in’. However, sometimes you just need to escape, move and be free. We incorporate the wider known Streetdance aka street, ‘an umbrella term encompassing a range of dance styles such as hip hop, funk, popping, locking, waving, krumping...’ and today combined with dancehall, African moves and varying cultural dances from around the world'. Quote by Kerry Maxwell, author of Brave New Words. Then... we do what we do. What we always do. We see what else we can do with our moves, like telling stories.
Lights! Camera! Action! What you got?

Music

Each production is shaped by the sound and aura created from the shared ideas. From the story, we analyse the mood, environment, time and tempo...
And with real, constructed or digital instruments, we create the scenes.
Songwriting and Arranging
Tracking and Editing
Mixing and Mastering
Music is a very personal element of self-expression and one should be allowed to draw an emotion in sound, for others to feel too. For the curious, we introduce participants in the creation of modern and traditional music, from music apps to woodwind instruments. We encourage a wide range of flexibility in alternative creations and help to develop individual and group projects.

Graffiti

Ambition is the desire to create the greatest masterpiece, to attempt to mimic the created piece. Art does not rise above the ambition which produces it.
Realisation is turning the ambition into reality. No matter how great the ambition, if the realisation falters, the art will be less.
Transformation is what happens when people experience it. Give children enough Art space to express themselves freely, and they will grow to enjoy Art. More, they may even discover a hidden self.
Express Yourself
Self-expression is fundamental to the development of young people’s identity and personhood. When youth feel like they are able to freely express themselves, they begin to explore personal interests and strengths. This helps them think about who they are and who they want to grow up to be. Art is crucial to the development of a young and growing mind. HTK ‘Open Arts Weekly’, SummerCamps and commissioned workshops allow all ages to share the same space together, whilst individual projects are being realised in the same safe and accessible environment.
